Troubleshooting

If the button above doesn't work, follow the steps below.

Android Users

On Android, the app can only be installed with Firefox, Chrome, Edge, Opera, and Samsung Internet Browser
Make sure you're not using private/incognito mode in the browser.
Open the app in one of the supported browsers and click on the "Options" button (usually this is the button with the 3 dots icon) and click on "Add to Home Screen".
Once the browser is done installing the app, you then see a new app icon on your home screen for CruiseCode Operator.

iOS Users

On iOS 16.3 and earlier, the app can only be installed with Safari.
On iOS 16.4 and later, the app can be installed from the Share menu in Safari, Chrome, Edge, Firefox, and Orion.
Make sure you're not using private/incognito mode in the browser.
Open the app in one of the supported browsers and click on the "Share" option and click on "Add to Home Screen".
Once the browser is done installing the app, you then see a new app icon on your home screen for CruiseCode Operator.
